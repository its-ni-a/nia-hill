Description

For my Typography 2 project, I designed a unique currency featuring subjects and landscapes relevant to the establishment and development of the United States. Departing from traditional American currency designs, I employed Khmer MN, Khmer Sangam MN, and Ayuthaya fonts to infuse a modern aesthetic. The front of the bill highlights influential persons of color such as Fred Begay, Katherine Johnson, Cesar Chavez, and Chien Shiung Wu, while the back showcases iconic American landscapes including the plains of Wyoming, the New York City skyline, the Arizona desert, and the Golden Gate Bridge in California. Each bill incorporates a color theme corresponding to the primary colors of red, blue, yellow, and green, symbolizing different aspects of American history and achievement.
